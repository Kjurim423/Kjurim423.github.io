Therapist Profile: Chris Hey

Education & Credentials

  • Masters in Clinical Psychology
    - University of Sydney, Class of 2010
  • Licensed Clinical Psychologist
    - Practicing since 2010

Areas of Specialization

  • Anxiety and Stress Management
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Family and Relationship Counseling
Professional Journey

Chris embarked on his journey to become a clinical psychologist driven by a profound desire to help individuals navigate their mental health challenges. After obtaining his Masters in Clinical Psychology from the University of Sydney, Chris has dedicated over a decade to practicing and refining his therapeutic techniques.

Distinguishing Practice Characteristics

Chris is known for his empathetic approach and his ability to make clients feel completely at ease from the very first session. His method combines evidence-based practices with a tailored approach that respects the unique circumstances of each client. Chris believes in not just addressing the symptoms but also exploring the root causes of distress to provide a comprehensive treatment plan.
